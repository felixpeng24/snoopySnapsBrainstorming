VARIANT 01

Peek-a-boo Teaser

Curtains idle with a soft breath. Every few seconds, Snoopy's face pokes through the gap — the Start button pulses as he peeks.

Playful · Alive VARIANT 02

Photo-Strip Capture

Press Start → curtains open, three flashes, Snoopy swaps pose each flash, then a polaroid strip slides out of the coin slot.

On-theme · Satisfying VARIANT 03

Mood Picker

Four mood chips (Cool, Sleepy, Party, Classic) recolor the curtain and swap which Snoopy appears behind it. One booth, four moods.

Replayable · Personal VARIANT 04

Peanuts Gang Rotation

Every press reveals a different friend — Cool Snoopy, Sleepy Snoopy, Classic Snoopy, Woodstock. Meet all four for a tiny surprise.

Collectible · Cozy VARIANT 05

Living Diorama

The booth sits in a quiet world. Woodstock flutters overhead trailing music notes, a doghouse peeks in from the corner, the background drifts slowly.
